# Audio Assets

Place your audio files here:

- `music.mid` or `music.mp3` - Background arcade music (looping)
- `bike_pedal.wav` - Bike pedaling sound (loop while moving)
- `pickup.wav` - Order pickup confirmation
- `delivery.wav` - Successful delivery chime
- `game_over.wav` - Game over sound
- `bounce.wav` - Obstacle collision

## Style Guide

- MIDI-style chiptune / arcade music
- Short, punchy sound effects
- 8-bit or 16-bit aesthetic
- Looping music should be seamless
